Personal

Hello, I’m Jeroen Harpe! A Perth based Full Stack Web Developer. I’m born and raised in The Netherlands where I got my degree in marketing and sales. After working 3 years as an account manager I decided it was time for a new adventure. I went on a world trip for 2 years and had the time of my life. During my trip I discovered it was time for a career change and I found a new passion in coding. I enjoy every line I code and love exploring new languages and frameworks.
